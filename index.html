Prayers of the Way

Traditional prayers to accompany your journey through the Stations.

Opening Prayer

My Lord Jesus Christ, You have made this journey to die for me with love unutterable, and I have so many times ungratefully abandoned You. But now I love You with my whole heart, and because I love You, I am sincerely sorry for having offended You.

Pardon me by Your mercy, and permit me to accompany You on this journey. You go to die for love of me; I wish, my beloved Redeemer, to die for love of You. My Jesus, I will live and die always united to You.

Amen.

At Each Station

V. We adore You, O Christ, and we bless You.

R. Because by Your holy cross You have redeemed the world.

The Fátima Prayer

O my Jesus, forgive us our sins, save us from the fires of hell, lead all souls to heaven, especially those who are in most need of Your mercy.

Amen.

Closing Prayer

Most merciful Jesus, Your agony in the garden, Your scourging at the pillar, Your crowning with thorns, and Your carrying of the cross — all this You endured for love of me.

Grant that I may carry my own cross with patience, and that I may have a share in the fruits of Your Redemption. May Your Passion be my strength, Your cross my comfort, and Your Resurrection my hope.

Amen.

The Final Blessing

Lord Jesus, we have walked with You the path of shadows. We have felt the weight of the timber and the sting of the thorns.

Grant that the memory of Your Passion may be our strength in weakness, Your love our light in darkness, and Your Resurrection our hope for eternity.

May we leave this place of prayer carrying Your peace into a restless world, becoming living icons of Your infinite mercy.

Amen.
